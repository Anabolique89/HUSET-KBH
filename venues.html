HUSET-KBH VENUES

Which event should you hold?

Are you missing a place for your big party, wedding, concert, conference, general meeting or something totally fifth? Hold your event in Huset-KBH! We offer rental of our premises for both private parties and corporate events. The house contains very different rooms, so we have something for every occasion. Here you will find beautiful surroundings for your big party in Salon K. A large bright room suitable for reception in the Ballroom. And of course our three scenes with professional technique, sound and light.

If you would like to know more about our party rooms and conference facilities, read more in the link below. Here you can find information about prices and other terms, and read more about the different premises. Did you know, for example, that Salon K has optimal acoustic conditions with classical concerts? Or that the House Cinema can be rented on an hourly basis for your private film screening?

Bastard Café – board games, coffee and hygge!

On the ground floor of Huset, you will find the popular board game café Bastard Café. Here you can play all kinds of board games and enjoy a nice creamy cup of coffee, a great micro brewed beer or get some goods for your tummy with the delicious fries and toast the café offers. Bastard Café has expanded with the basement. Here you can also enjoy all types of board games but you can also experience a bit more of a bar atmosphere and try out some of their amazing gin! Teaterhuset

We live at Huset-KBH, but we are our own little unit. The members uses our space to rehearse, experiment and create shows. We have more than 7 rehearsal spaces together with the two stages Vox and Xenon. Our members creates approximately 16 shows a year on these stages. Every year in august we make the Vilskud Theater festival. As a member of Teaterhuset you can use our spaces to rehearse, produce a show, rehearse for the theater schools, be a part of creating new activities or be in the mangement for new initiativs as Vildskud & Stage.
Ask for more info!

Musikcaféen – The alternative music stage of Copenhagen

On the third floor of Huset you will find Musikcaféen, wich is the biggest and oldest stage of Huset. This stage presents up-and-coming artist in rock, pop and alternative music. The stage has in more than 40 years been the place where you’ll first meet the newest artist. This stage has provided the foundation for koncerts with DAD, Red Hot Chili Peppers, The William Blakes, Oh Land, Rumour Said Fire, Dagny and Dizzy Mizz Lizzy who took the title of winners at DM in Rock in 1993. It is also possible to rent these rooms for private events : Ask for more info!
